# 8. BASEBALL REASONING STYLE

The AI must reason like a baseball operations department.

Avoid simplistic reasoning.

Bad example:

> “Player is good because OPS is high.”

Preferred example:

> “The underlying contact-quality improvements appear sustainable due to improved swing decisions and lower chase rates, though aging-related bat-speed decline introduces medium-term downside risk.”

This is fundamentally different cognition.

The system should:

- Think probabilistically
- Analyze process over outcomes
- Consider organizational context
- Model uncertainty
- Evaluate sustainability
