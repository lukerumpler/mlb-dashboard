# MLB Intelligence Terminal - Master Build Requirements

## Core Vision
Build "The Bloomberg Terminal for Baseball" - an elite-level baseball operations intelligence platform combining scouting, analytics, projections, roster construction, and player development.

## Primary Tabs Required
1. Dashboard - Executive command center
2. Personnel - Roster management & player operations
3. Analysis - Quantitative baseball intelligence
4. Player Profile - Complete baseball intelligence dossier
5. Top Prospects - MLB scouting & player development database
6. Scout Notes - Scouting observations & reports
7. Reports - Generated analysis & insights
8. Strategy - Organizational strategy & planning
9. Analytics - Advanced statistical analysis
10. Schedule - Team schedule & matchups
11. Settings - User preferences & configuration
12. About Me - System information

## Design Principles
- **Feel**: Command center, military intelligence dashboard, professional analytics terminal
- **NOT**: Playful, gimmicky, consumer-focused, cartoonish
- **IS**: Dark, premium, modern, tactical, sharp, sophisticated, efficient

## Color System
- **Navy/Charcoal/Slate**: Primary dark backgrounds
- **Orange**: Active systems, selected navigation, above-average metrics
- **Red**: Elite performance, danger, critical intelligence, severe weaknesses
- **Blue**: Below-average metrics, cold streaks, weak tools
- **Green**: Value, surplus value, positive trends, acquisition opportunities
- **Gray**: Neutral data, average metrics, secondary information

## Required Hitting Metrics
### Traditional Advanced
WAR, wRC+, OPS+, ISO, BABIP, BB%, K%, K-BB%, Contact%, Chase%, Swing%, O-Swing%, Z-Swing%, O-Contact%, Z-Contact%, SwStr%

### Statcast Metrics
xwOBA, xBA, xSLG, xISO, EV, Max EV, Launch Angle, Sweet Spot%, Barrel%, Hard Hit%, Sprint Speed, Bat Speed, Squared-Up%, Blast Rate

### Contact Quality
wOBAcon, xwOBAcon, SLGcon, Pull Fly Ball%, Pull Air%, GB%, FB%, LD%, Oppo%, HR/FB

### Swing Decision
Swing Decision Score, Attack Zone Efficiency, Decision Value, Two Strike Contact Ability, Early Count Aggression, Swing Path Efficiency

### Splits
vs RHP, vs LHP, Home/Away, High Leverage, RISP, Day/Night, Pitch Type Splits, Zone Splits, Velocity Band Splits

## Required Pitching Metrics
### Core Metrics
ERA, FIP, xFIP, SIERA, WHIP, K/9, BB/9, HR/9, K-BB%, CSW%, Chase Rate

### Statcast Pitch Metrics
Velocity, Spin Rate, IVB, HB, Extension, Release Height, Release Side, VAA, Pitch Shape, Movement Profiles

### Stuff Models
Stuff+, Pitching+, Location+, Command+, Arsenal+, Fastball Quality Score, Breaking Ball Effectiveness, Changeup Separation Score

### Expected Metrics
xERA, xBA Allowed, xSLG Allowed, xwOBA Allowed, Barrel% Allowed, Hard Hit% Allowed

### Arsenal Intelligence
Pitch Usage%, Run Value by Pitch, Putaway Rate, Whiff Rate by Pitch, Pitch Tunneling, Release Consistency, Sequencing Efficiency
